    First Tech Challenge

    FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an exciting robotics competition for students in grades 7–12. Participants design, build, and program robots to perform specific tasks. The challenge encourages hands-on learning, teamwork, and innovation. Using a reusable platform powered by Android technology, students gain engineering experience and develop problem-solving skills. FTC events culminate in local and regional competitions, with teams vying for awards and a chance to qualify for the FIRST Championship. Whether you're into coding, metalworking, or public speaking, FTC offers a place for everyone to explore STEM and robotics! Learn more on the [official FIRST website].


    In the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), a new challenge game is introduced each season. This keeps the competition fresh and exciting, encouraging participants to adapt, innovate, and apply their skills to new scenarios. Whether it’s navigating obstacles, collecting objects, or performing specific tasks, the annual challenge game provides a dynamic platform for students to showcase their creativity and problem-solving abilities. So, each year, teams eagerly await the unveiling of the latest challenge, ready to tackle it head-on!
